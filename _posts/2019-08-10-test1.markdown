Try to solve the equation $$x+1=2$$:
<center>
$$
\begin{equation}
	\begin{aligned}
		x+1&=2 \\
		x+1-1&=2-1 \\
		x&=1\\
	\end{aligned}
\end{equation}
$$
</center>
Then we get $$x=1$$.
